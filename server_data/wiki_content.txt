[Account]
Your account is the foundation of character progression and overall history.

[Account/Leveling]
Account Leveling lets you advance individual classes (combat and crafting) instead of a single account level. There is no level cap, and each level grants Upgrade Points to enhance skills, abilities, and professions.

[Account/Achievements]
The Achievement System rewards points for milestones like item collection and PVP victories. Achievements can grant titles and unique items.

[Account/Rank]
Ranking shows your position among players or guilds.

[Economy]
The economy ties everything together: resources are gathered, stores are supplied, and player trading shapes demand.

[Economy/Bank]
The bank keeps your gold safe with a storage fee, protecting it from loss on death.

[Economy/Stores]
Stores across the world sell different assortments. Buying and selling creates demand and keeps trade moving.

[Economy/Auctions]
Auctions allow players to sell any item at their own price, enabling player-driven trade.

[Economy/Weapons]
Every weapon is unique and can be used by mobs, NPCs, and players. Weapons can be enchanted, dropped, or shared.

[Economy/Housing]
Maps contain houses and guild houses. Each can be purchased, decorated, and used to grow plants.

[Items]
Items are found, crafted, or earned. Rare items can be unique to their owners.

[Items/Crafting]
Crafting turns resources into items, from iron ingots to vampire armor. It takes time but pays off.

[Items/Enchantments]
Enchantments improve item attributes and require materials and gold.

[Character]
Classes, skills, and professions define your character.

[Character/Classes]
Class specialization lets you build a strong fighter or a supportive healer. Skill points shape your role and playstyle.

[Character/Skills]
Use a mix of general and class-specific skills. Activate with /useskill <number>.

[Character/Quests]
Quests provide unique NPC tasks, rewards, and activity points you can spend on valuable items.

[Character/Mining]
Mining is a core profession: ore is essential for crafting.

[Character/Farming]
Farming is the simplest profession and rewards you with gold for harvesting crops.

[Management]
Menus for account and character management.

[Management/Personal Menu]
Your personal menu for controlling professions, classes, and core settings.

[Management/Upgrades]
Upgrades and professions let you customize classes and skills in detail. You can change class outside of combat.

[Management/Equipment]
Equipment shows what you have equipped and lets you swap items.

[Management/Modules]
Modules work like equipment slots for special items.

[Management/Inventory]
Inventory management lets you recycle materials, discard items, or prepare them for sale.

[Management/Social]
Social and group menus handle parties, guilds, and mailbox deliveries.

[Management/Dungeons]
Dungeons are dangerous raids for solo or group play.

[Management/Group]
Groups let you team up without damaging each other.

[Management/Guild]
Guilds allow you to manage upgrades, the guild house, and interactions like wars.

[Management/Guild Finder]
Use the guild finder to search and join a guild.

[Management/Mailbox]
Your mailbox stores delivered items and messages.

[Management/Settings]
Settings let you change language, titles, and gameplay-related options.

[Management/Journal]
The journal tracks account history and quest logs.
